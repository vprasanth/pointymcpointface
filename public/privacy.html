Privacy Policy for Ack

Effective date: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ack ("the App") handles information when installed in a Slack workspace.

Information We Process

  • Slack workspace identifiers (for example, team and channel IDs).
  • Slack user identifiers for users who give or receive points.
  • Award metadata such as giver, receiver, timestamp, and optional reason text.
  • OAuth installation data required to operate the Slack app.

How We Use Information

  • To process @user++ awards and /points commands.
  • To maintain points totals, leaderboard results, and award history.
  • To operate app installation/authentication and support reliability/diagnostics.

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in a Postgres database operated by the app owner.
  • OAuth installation data is encrypted at rest.
  • Access to production systems should be limited to authorized operators.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is only shared as needed to operate the App, such as hosting, database, and infrastructure providers used by the app owner.

Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as needed to provide App functionality and meet operational or legal obligations. Workspace admins may request deletion of workspace data.

Your Choices

  • Workspace admins can uninstall the App at any time.
  • Workspace admins can request deletion of stored data for their workspace.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: support@ack.example

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Additional support information is available at /support.
