Ack for Slack

Ack is a lightweight recognition app for Slack. Team members award points with @user++ and use /points to view totals, history, and leaderboards.

Install Ack in Slack

How Ack works

  • Give points in messages with @user++ plus an optional reason.
  • Query points with /points, including leaderboard, stats, and history.
  • Supports public and private channels by default.
